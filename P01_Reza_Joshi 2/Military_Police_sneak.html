Secret Training: Caught in the Dark

The thrill of the forbidden fills you as you slip on your ODM gear beneath a moonlit alley. You practice silent maneuvers, climb low rooftops, and imagine joining the Scouts on daring raids. For a few stolen hours, you feel alive.

Then a lantern catches the gleam of metal. A voice, not yours, orders you to freeze. The Military Police do not tolerate desertion from duty or unauthorized training, especially within the capital.

The Consequences

You are detained. The captain reads the accusations in a tone colder than the night air: unauthorized possession of equipment, breach of duty, and endangering the regiment. Your fate is sealed, stripped of rank and dismissed to a life far from the glory you sought.

There are no cheers for heroism here, only the echo of a choice that cost you everything you had worked for.

Outcome: Lose Ending, caught training in secret. Dismissed from the Military Police.
