Congratulations, recruit! You have decided to join the Scouts, the bravest division in all of humanity. The Scouts venture beyond the safety of the walls to learn more about the world and protect the people.

Scout Regiment: Training Test

The morning sun shines over the Scout Regiment training field. Recruits gather, nervous but excited. Today is the day you prove whether you have what it takes to join humanity's bravest soldiers.

Your instructor, Commander Hange Zoe, shouts, “Listen up, recruits! Today's ODM gear test will separate the strong from the weak. Stay calm, balance your weight, and remember, momentum is everything!”

Your Test Begins

You attach your Omni-Directional Mobility (ODM) gear cables to the wooden poles and take a deep breath. The ground feels steady under your boots, but your hands are trembling. Around you, other recruits are already swinging through the air. This is your moment. Will you pass, or will your nerves get the best of you?

What will you do?

  • Focus, trust your balance, and pass the test.
  • Lose your grip and fail the test.

Tip: Choose carefully, as your performance determines your fate in the scouts.
